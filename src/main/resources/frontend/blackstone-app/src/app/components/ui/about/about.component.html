Welcome

Blackstone Cafe Café is Canberra’s newest dining establishment. Blackstone Cafe provides for all your dining requirements. Open early from 7am for breakfast or coffee.

Lunch at Blackstone Cafe is whatever you choose it to be: whether it is a healthy, tasty takeaway meal or a gourmet lunch accompanied with your favourite wine or beer. Either way, Blackstone Cafe’s commitment is to provide quality food and excellent customer service in a relaxed and friendly ambience. Blackstone Cafe also offers an extensive and flexible catering menu. We are able to provide food of the finest quality and presentation delivered to your location. We know that your time is valued, so why not order online. Functions are also a feature at Blackstone Cafe Café. Whatever your function style or theme we are happy to tailor a package to suit your needs
