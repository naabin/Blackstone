About us

Blackstone Café is a modern eatery located on the Ground Floor of the Geoscience Australia Building in Symonston. The café is open Monday to Friday from 7 am to 4 pm.

Lunch at Blackstone Cafe is whatever you choose it to be: whether it is a healthy, tasty takeaway meal or a dine-in gourmet lunch, the options are varied. Blackstone Cafe’s commitment is to provide quality food and excellent customer service in a relaxed and friendly ambience. Blackstone Cafe also offers an extensive and flexible catering menu. We are able to provide food of the finest quality and presentation delivered to your location. We know that your time is valued, so why not order online.
